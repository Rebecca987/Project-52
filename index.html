Camel

Interesting facts about camel.

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants
  • There are one humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels

Chamaleon

Interesting facts about chamaleon.

  • There are over 200 species of chameleons
  • Chameleons have the ability to change color
  • Usually, chameleons change color to reflect their moods

Flying Cicada

Interesting facts about flying cicada.

  • There are approximately 2,500 species of cicada around the globe
  • Cicadas do not bite or sting
  • Cicadas are sometimes called "locusts"

Panda

Interesting facts about panda.

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day
  • Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit

Pigeon

Interesting facts about pigeon.

  • Pigeons are highly sociable animals
  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  • Both female and male pigeons share responsibility of caring for and raising young

Teddy

Interesting facts about teddy.

  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing
  • Bears care deeply about family members
  • Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals
